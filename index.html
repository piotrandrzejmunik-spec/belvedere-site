FAQ

⏱️ How long does a project take?

Most projects take 3–10 days depending on size and complexity. Larger full-garden renovations can take longer, but we’ll always give you a clear schedule.

🧩 Do you offer full garden redesign?

Yes. We can handle everything from initial design and layout to hard landscaping, planting and finishing details.

🌧️ Do you work in all weather?

We operate year-round unless conditions are unsafe or would compromise the quality of the finish. If we need to pause, we’ll explain why and what it means for the schedule.

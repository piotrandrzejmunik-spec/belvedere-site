FAQ

Questions that usually come up before we start.

How far in advance should we book?

It depends on the season and project size. For full renovations, 4–8 weeks’ notice is ideal, but we’ll always tell you honestly what’s possible.

Do you handle everything or just parts of the job?

We can handle the full outdoor scope — from clearing and groundworks to patios, decking, fencing and finishing details.

What if the weather is bad?

We work year-round, but we won’t pour concrete or lay paving in conditions that would compromise the finish. If we need to pause, we’ll explain why and what it means for the schedule.
